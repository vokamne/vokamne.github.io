About

Volodymyr Cherepovskyi — Kingston University

Building worlds,
one level at a time.

I'm a Game Development student at Kingston University, driven by the idea that great games are built by people who genuinely care about what they're making.

What I love most is the process — watching something grow from nothing into a world with its own feel and character. Whether it's writing code, shaping a level, or working through a problem with a team, I'm at my best when I'm building something that means something.

I enjoy collaborative development — the back and forth, the shared decisions, the moment when separate pieces finally come together. I work primarily in Unreal Engine 5 with Blueprint and C++, and I'm always looking to grow across the full spectrum of game development.
